About

关于

Jieyu Pan is a Chinese cellist and composer currently pursuing her master study. She plays as a soloist and in ensembles and orchestras for music of different genres and styles. She found her passion in composition through these rich performing experiences and discovered her particular compositional interest in film score and other visual media. Pan hopes to express and convey the beauty of music to her audiences and herself, as well as our living world.

潘杰妤是一名中国大提琴演奏家与作曲家，目前正在攻读硕士学位。她活跃于独奏、合奏及管弦乐团等多种音乐形式的演出中。通过这些丰富的演奏经历，她发现了自己在影视配乐及视听媒介创作上的特别兴趣。她希望通过音乐，传达人与世界的美。
