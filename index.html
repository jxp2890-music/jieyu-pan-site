About / 关于

Jieyu Pan is a Chinese cellist and composer currently pursuing her master study at the University of Miami. She plays as a soloist and in ensembles and orchestras for music of different genres and styles. She found her passion in composition through these rich performing experiences and discovered her particular compositional interest in film score and other visual media. Pan hopes to express and convey the beauty of music to her audiences and herself, as well as our living world.

潘杰妤是一位来自中国的大提琴演奏家与作曲家，目前就读于迈阿密大学音乐学院硕士课程。她活跃于独奏、室内乐及各类风格的乐团演出中， 并在丰富的演奏经历中发现了对作曲的热情，尤其专注于影视配乐及其他视觉媒体音乐的创作。 她希望通过音乐传达与表达世界与自我之美，将情感与听觉体验融为一体。
