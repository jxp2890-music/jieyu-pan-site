Grow (\uc0\u33804 \u33469 )

\

Piece for Piano & Cello

\

\ This piece portrays the composer's journey and growth along the path of composition study. \ Narrative-driven, using documenting personal growth as creative motive, metaphorically comparing \ the yearning for composition to a sprouting seed that progresses through four stages: germination, growth, \ faltering, and reshaping. A touch of unconventional sounds is employed to express and simulate the sounds \ of the natural environment.\
